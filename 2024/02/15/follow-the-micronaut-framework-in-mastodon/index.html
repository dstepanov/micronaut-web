Follow the Micronaut Framework in Mastodon

The Micronaut Foundation is happy to announce that you can follow us on Mastodon at fosstodon.org/@micronaut.

With the Micronaut framework being an open-source project, we chose Fosstodon as the Mastodon instance.

Fosstodon is a Mastodon instance that is open to anyone who is interested in technology; particularly free & open source software.

Mastodon is an additional avenue for more engagement with the community around the Framework. You can continue to follow @micronautfw in X, formerly Twitter, and join the Micronaut Discord community to stay in touch.
