We are super pumped to be working in partnership with our friends on the AWS Lambda team to deliver a free, in-person Micronaut workshop at Amazon HQ in Dublin, Ireland!

This free workshop is an event you won’t want to miss! It’s scheduled for Friday, May 27th and taught by Sergio del Amo, the Micronaut Product Development Lead at Object Computing. Sergio has been a core committer to the Micronaut framework since its inception in 2017. As the Developer Advocate for the Grails and Micronaut Foundations, he hosts [Micronaut Live](https://www.twitch.tv/micronautfw) on Twitch and the [Micronaut Podcast](https://micronautpodcast.com/).

To reserve your spot, DM (direct message) [Mark Sailes](https://twitter.com/MarkSailes3) on Twitter. Seating is limited, so please register ASAP to guarantee your spot!

## Details

### Jumpstart Your Micronaut Applications with AWS Lambda

**Date:** May 27th, 2022

**Time:** 9:30 a.m. to 5:00 p.m. IST

**Location:** Amazon Ireland Shannon Building Burlington Road D04 HH21 Dublin Ireland

### Topics Covered

- Micronaut framework
- [AWS Lambda](https://aws.amazon.com/lambda/)
- [GraalVM](https://graalvm.org)
- S3, API Gateway, DynamoDB integrations
- and more!

We hope to see you there!
