The MQTT protocol provides a lightweight method of carrying out messaging using a publish/subscribe model. These characteristics make it suitable for messaging between IoT devices, such as low-power sensors.

In this complimentary webinar, Sergio del Amo introduces the Micronaut MQTT module and demonstrates how to publish and subscribe to MQTT topics from a Micronaut application.

Watch the video to discover how the Micronaut framework can be part of your IoT projects, learn how to get started with Micronaut MQTT, and get an introduction to Eclipse Mosquitto, a popular message broker implementing the MQTT protocol.
