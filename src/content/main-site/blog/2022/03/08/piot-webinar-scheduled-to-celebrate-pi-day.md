In honor of Pi Day, let’s talk about [Micronaut MQTT](https://micronaut-projects.github.io/micronaut-mqtt/latest/guide/), a Micronaut module that’s a perfect fit for your [Raspberry Pi](https://www.raspberrypi.org) + IoT devices!

The MQTT protocol provides a lightweight method of carrying out messaging using a publish/subscribe model. These characteristics make it suitable for messaging between IoT devices, such as low-power sensors.

Discover how the Micronaut framework can be part of your IoT projects, learn how to get started with Micronaut MQTT, and see how to use it in combination with [Eclipse Mosquitto](https://mosquitto.org), a popular message broker implementing the MQTT protocol.

**Date:** March 14, 2022

**Time:** 11 a.m. to noon CDT
