This 12-hour workshop arms students with everything they need to immediately start building applications using the Micronaut framework.

- 🗓 Dates: March 7 to March 10
- ⌛️ Duration: 12 hours (3 hours per day for 4 days)
- ⏰ Time: 8:00 a.m. to 11:00 a.m. CST
- 📍 Location: Online
- 👨🏻‍🏫 Instructor: Zachary Klein
- 💵 Registration Fee: $200.00 USD

[Learn more and enroll](https://objectcomputing.com/services/training/catalog/micronaut-training/micronaut-essentials)

## [Jumpstart Your Micronaut Applications with AWS Lambda](https://objectcomputing.com/services/training/catalog/micronaut-training/micronaut-aws-lambda)

In this 9-hour workshop, JVM developers learn how to develop and deploy Micronaut applications to [AWS Lambda](https://aws.amazon.com/lambda/).

- 🗓 Dates: March 28, 29, and 30, 2022
- ⌛️ Duration: 9 hours (3 hours per day for 3 days)
- ⏰ Time: 9:00 a.m. to 12:00 p.m. CDT
- 📍 Location: Online
- 👨🏻‍🏫 Instructor: Sergio del Amo Caballero
- 💵 Registration Fee: $200.00 USD

[Learn more and enroll](https://objectcomputing.com/services/training/catalog/micronaut-training/micronaut-aws-lambda)
