The Micronaut Foundation is excited to announce the release of Micronaut framework 4.1.6!

It contains a patch release of [Micronaut Core](https://github.com/micronaut-projects/micronaut-core/releases/v4.1.11) and upgrades [Micronaut Picocli](https://github.com/micronaut-projects/micronaut-picocli/releases/tag/v5.0.2), [Micronaut MQTT](https://github.com/micronaut-projects/micronaut-mqtt/releases/tag/v3.0.2), and [Micronaut Reactor](https://github.com/micronaut-projects/micronaut-reactor/releases/tag/v3.0.5).

## Next Steps

If you haven’t yet updated to [Micronaut framework 4](https://micronaut.io/2023/07/14/micronaut-framework-4-0-0-released/), this is an excellent opportunity to do so!

Please feel free to [reach out to us](https://micronaut.io/support/) if you need any assistance.
