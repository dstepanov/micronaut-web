The Micronaut Foundation is excited to announce the release of Micronaut framework 4.1.5!

It contains a patch release of [Micronaut Core](https://github.com/micronaut-projects/micronaut-core/releases/v4.1.10) and upgrades [Micronaut AWS](https://github.com/micronaut-projects/micronaut-aws/releases/tag/v4.0.7), [Micronaut RxJava 3](https://github.com/micronaut-projects/micronaut-rxjava3/releases/tag/v3.0.2), [Micronaut Discovery Client](https://github.com/micronaut-projects/micronaut-discovery-client/releases/tag/v4.0.3), [Micronaut Reactor](https://github.com/micronaut-projects/micronaut-reactor/releases/tag/v3.0.3), and [Micronaut Object Storage](https://github.com/micronaut-projects/micronaut-object-storage/releases/tag/v2.0.4).

Moreover, it updates to [Netty 4.1.100](https://netty.io/news/2023/10/10/4-1-100-Final.html).

## NEXT STEPS

If you haven’t yet updated to [Micronaut framework 4](https://micronaut.io/2023/07/14/micronaut-framework-4-0-0-released/), this is an excellent opportunity to do so!

Please feel free to [reach out to us](https://micronaut.io/support/) if you need any assistance.
