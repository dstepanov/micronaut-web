The Micronaut Foundation is excited to announce the release of Micronaut framework 4.1.4!

It contains a patch release of [Micronaut Core](https://github.com/micronaut-projects/micronaut-core/releases/v4.1.9) and upgrades [Micronaut Serialization](https://github.com/micronaut-projects/micronaut-serialization/releases/tag/v2.2.6), [Micronaut AWS](https://github.com/micronaut-projects/micronaut-aws/releases/tag/v4.0.5), [Micronaut Email](https://github.com/micronaut-projects/micronaut-email/releases/tag/v2.2.1), [Micronaut Data](https://github.com/micronaut-projects/micronaut-data/releases/tag/v4.1.4), [Micronaut Maven](https://github.com/micronaut-projects/micronaut-maven-plugin/releases/tag/v4.0.7), [Micronaut SQL](https://github.com/micronaut-projects/micronaut-sql/releases/tag/v5.0.3), and [Micronaut Discovery Client](https://github.com/micronaut-projects/micronaut-discovery-client/releases/tag/v4.0.2).

## Next Steps

If you haven’t yet updated to [Micronaut framework 4](https://micronaut.io/2023/07/14/micronaut-framework-4-0-0-released/), this is an excellent opportunity to do so!

Please feel free to [reach out to us](https://micronaut.io/support/) if you need any assistance.
