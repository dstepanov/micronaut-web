Thank you to everyone who joined us for our first Micronaut Town Hall Meeting of 2023! For those of you who missed the live event, the recording is embedded below.

During our meeting, we provided an update on the latest advancements with the Micronaut framework, including a look at what is coming soon with Micronaut 4. Additionally we were introduced to the Unity Foundation and learned about an exciting partnership between Unity, the Micronaut Foundation, and St. Louis University students. The latest news, events, and sponsors of the Micronaut Foundation were shared, as well as some great live discussions and Q&A with our panel.

A special thanks to our fantastic panel of experts: Sergio del Amo, Graeme Rocher, Steve Totten, Kate Holdener, and Jen Wiese

Till next time!

— Jen Wiese
