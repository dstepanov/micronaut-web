The Micronaut Foundation is excited to announce the release of Micronaut framework 4.0.5!

It contains patch releases of [Micronaut Core](https://github.com/micronaut-projects/micronaut-core/compare/v4.0.3...v4.0.5) and it upgrades [Micronaut Cassandra](https://github.com/micronaut-projects/micronaut-cassandra/releases/tag/v6.0.2), [Micronaut MicroStream](https://github.com/micronaut-projects/micronaut-microstream/releases/tag/v2.0.2), [Micronaut Security](https://github.com/micronaut-projects/micronaut-security/releases/tag/v4.0.4), [Micronaut Liquibase](https://github.com/micronaut-projects/micronaut-liquibase/releases/tag/v6.0.2), [Micronaut Flyway](https://github.com/micronaut-projects/micronaut-flyway/releases/tag/v6.0.2), [Micronaut GCP](https://github.com/micronaut-projects/micronaut-gcp/releases/tag/v5.0.4), [Micronaut AWS](https://github.com/micronaut-projects/micronaut-aws/releases/tag/v4.0.2), and [Micronaut Servlet](https://github.com/micronaut-projects/micronaut-servlet/releases/tag/v4.0.3) modules to patch releases.

## NEXT STEPS

If you haven’t yet updated to [Micronaut framework 4](https://micronaut.io/2023/07/14/micronaut-framework-4-0-0-released/), this is an excellent opportunity to do so!

Please feel free to [reach out to us](https://micronaut.io/support/) if you need any assistance.
