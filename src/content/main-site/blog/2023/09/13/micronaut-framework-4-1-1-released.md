The Micronaut Foundation is excited to announce the release of Micronaut framework 4.1.1!

It contains patch releases of [Micronaut Core](https://github.com/micronaut-projects/micronaut-core/releases/v4.1.4), and it upgrades [Micronaut Oracle Cloud](https://github.com/micronaut-projects/micronaut-oracle-cloud/releases/tag/v3.0.4), [Micronaut AOT](https://github.com/micronaut-projects/micronaut-aot/releases/tag/v2.1.1), [Micronaut Data](https://github.com/micronaut-projects/micronaut-data/releases/tag/v4.1.2), [Micronaut Kafka](https://github.com/micronaut-projects/micronaut-kafka/releases/tag/v5.1.2), [Micronaut Kotlin](https://github.com/micronaut-projects/micronaut-kotlin/releases/tag/v4.0.2), [Micronaut Test](https://github.com/micronaut-projects/micronaut-test/releases/tag/v4.0.2), [Micronaut Validation](https://github.com/micronaut-projects/micronaut-validation/releases/tag/v4.0.3), and [Micronaut Multi-Tenancy](https://github.com/micronaut-projects/micronaut-multitenancy/releases/tag/v5.0.3) modules to patch releases.

## NEXT STEPS

If you haven’t yet updated to [Micronaut framework 4](https://micronaut.io/2023/07/14/micronaut-framework-4-0-0-released/), this is an excellent opportunity to do so!

Please feel free to [reach out to us](https://micronaut.io/support/) if you need any assistance.
