## The Micronaut Foundation is excited to announce the release of Micronaut framework 4.5.1!

It contains patch releases of [Micronaut Core](https://github.com/micronaut-projects/micronaut-core/releases/tag/v4.5.4), [Micronaut Micrometer](https://github.com/micronaut-projects/micronaut-micrometer/releases/tag/v5.7.1), [Micronaut OpenAPI](https://github.com/micronaut-projects/micronaut-openapi/releases/tag/v6.11.1), [Micronaut Security](https://github.com/micronaut-projects/micronaut-security/releases/tag/v4.9.1), [Micronaut SourceGen](https://github.com/micronaut-projects/micronaut-sourcegen/releases/tag/v1.1.1), [Micronaut Data](https://github.com/micronaut-projects/micronaut-data/releases/tag/v4.8.4), [Micronaut Reactor](https://github.com/micronaut-projects/micronaut-reactor/releases/tag/v3.4.1), [Micronaut Test Resources](https://github.com/micronaut-projects/micronaut-test-resources/releases/tag/v2.5.4), [Micronaut Test](https://github.com/micronaut-projects/micronaut-test/releases/tag/v4.3.0), [Micronaut gRPC](https://github.com/micronaut-projects/micronaut-micrometer/releases/tag/v4.6.0), [Micronaut Validation](https://github.com/micronaut-projects/micronaut-validation/releases/tag/v4.6.1), and [Micronaut Views.](https://github.com/micronaut-projects/micronaut-views/releases/tag/v5.4.1)

## NEXT STEPS

If you haven’t yet updated to [Micronaut framework 4](https://micronaut.io/2023/07/14/micronaut-framework-4-0-0-released/), this is an excellent opportunity to do so!

Please feel free to [reach out to us](https://micronaut.io/support/) if you need any assistance.
