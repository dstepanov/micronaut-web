The Micronaut Foundation is excited to announce the release of Micronaut framework 4.2.4!

It contains patch releases of [Micronaut Core](https://github.com/micronaut-projects/micronaut-core/releases/v4.2.4), [Micronaut AWS](https://github.com/micronaut-projects/micronaut-aws/releases/tag/v4.1.6), [Micronaut Flyway](https://github.com/micronaut-projects/micronaut-flyway/releases/tag/v6.2.2), [Micronaut JAX-RS](https://github.com/micronaut-projects/micronaut-jaxrs/releases/tag/v4.2.1), [Micronaut JMS](https://github.com/micronaut-projects/micronaut-jms/releases/tag/v3.1.1), [Micronaut MicroStream](https://github.com/micronaut-projects/micronaut-microstream/releases/tag/v2.2.1), [Micronaut MQTT](https://github.com/micronaut-projects/micronaut-mqtt/releases/tag/v3.1.1), and [Micronaut Servlet](https://github.com/micronaut-projects/micronaut-servlet/releases/tag/v4.2.2).

## NEXT STEPS

If you haven’t yet updated to [Micronaut framework 4](https://micronaut.io/2023/07/14/micronaut-framework-4-0-0-released/), this is an excellent opportunity to do so!

Please feel free to [reach out to us](https://micronaut.io/support/) if you need any assistance.
