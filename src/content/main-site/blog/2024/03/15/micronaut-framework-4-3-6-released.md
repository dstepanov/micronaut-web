The Micronaut Foundation is excited to announce the release of Micronaut framework 4.3.6!

It contains patch releases of [Micronaut Core](https://github.com/micronaut-projects/micronaut-core/releases/tag/v4.3.11), [Micronaut Serialization](https://github.com/micronaut-projects/micronaut-serialization/releases/tag/v2.8.2), [Micronaut Azure](https://github.com/micronaut-projects/micronaut-azure/releases/tag/v5.3.1), [Micronaut RxJava3](https://github.com/micronaut-projects/micronaut-rxjava3/releases/tag/v3.2.1), and [Micronaut Validation](https://github.com/micronaut-projects/micronaut-validation/releases/tag/v4.4.4)

## NEXT STEPS

If you haven’t yet updated to [Micronaut framework 4](https://micronaut.io/2023/07/14/micronaut-framework-4-0-0-released/), this is an excellent opportunity to do so!

Please feel free to [reach out to us](https://micronaut.io/support/) if you need any assistance.
