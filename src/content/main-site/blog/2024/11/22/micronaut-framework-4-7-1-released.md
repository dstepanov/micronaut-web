The Micronaut Foundation is excited to announce the release of Micronaut framework 4.7.1!

It contains patch releases of [Core](https://github.com/micronaut-projects/micronaut-core/releases/tag/v4.7.6), [Security](https://github.com/micronaut-projects/micronaut-security/releases/tag/v4.11.2), [JaxRS](https://github.com/micronaut-projects/micronaut-jaxrs/releases/tag/v4.7.2), [Data](https://github.com/micronaut-projects/micronaut-data/releases/tag/v4.10.4), [OpenAPI](https://github.com/micronaut-projects/micronaut-openapi/releases/tag/v6.13.2), and [Micrometer.](https://github.com/micronaut-projects/micronaut-micrometer/releases/tag/v5.9.3)

## NEXT STEPS

If you haven’t yet updated to [Micronaut framework 4](https://micronaut.io/2023/07/14/micronaut-framework-4-0-0-released/), this is an excellent opportunity to do so!

Please feel free to [reach out to us](https://micronaut.io/support/) if you need any assistance.
