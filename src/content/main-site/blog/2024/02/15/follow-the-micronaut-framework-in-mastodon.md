The Micronaut Foundation is happy to announce that you can follow us on [Mastodon](https://joinmastodon.org/) at [fosstodon.org/@micronaut](https://fosstodon.org/@micronaut).

With the Micronaut framework being an open-source project, we chose [Fosstodon](https://fosstodon.org/) as the Mastodon instance.

> Fosstodon is a Mastodon instance that is open to anyone who is interested in technology; particularly free & open source software.

Mastodon is an additional avenue for more engagement with the community around the Framework. You can continue to follow [@micronautfw in X, formerly Twitter](https://twitter.com/micronautfw), and join the [Micronaut Discord community](https://discord.com/invite/9xRFsHv98T) to stay in touch.
