The Micronaut Foundation is excited to announce the release of Micronaut framework 4.3.2!

It contains patch releases of [Micronaut Core](https://github.com/micronaut-projects/micronaut-core/releases/tag/v4.3.6), [Micronaut Spring](https://github.com/micronaut-projects/micronaut-spring/releases/tag/v5.4.1), [Micronaut Tracing](https://github.com/micronaut-projects/micronaut-tracing/releases/tag/v6.4.1), [Micronaut SQL](https://github.com/micronaut-projects/micronaut-sql/releases/tag/v5.5.1), [Micronaut Cache](https://github.com/micronaut-projects/micronaut-cache/releases/tag/v4.2.2), and [Micronaut Security](https://github.com/micronaut-projects/micronaut-security/releases/tag/v4.6.3).

Moreover, it updates to [Netty 4.1.107](https://netty.io/news/2024/02/13/4-1-107-Final.html).

### NEXT STEPS

If you haven’t yet updated to [Micronaut framework 4](https://micronaut.io/2023/07/14/micronaut-framework-4-0-0-released/), this is an excellent opportunity to do so!

Please feel free to [reach out to us](https://micronaut.io/support/) if you need any assistance.
