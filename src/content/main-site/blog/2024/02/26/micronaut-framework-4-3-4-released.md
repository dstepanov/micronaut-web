The Micronaut Foundation is excited to announce the release of Micronaut framework 4.3.4!

It contains patch releases of [Micronaut Core](https://github.com/micronaut-projects/micronaut-core/releases/tag/v4.3.9), [Micronaut Security](https://github.com/micronaut-projects/micronaut-security/releases/tag/v4.6.5), and [Micronaut AWS](https://github.com/micronaut-projects/micronaut-aws/releases/tag/v4.4.2).

## NEXT STEPS

If you haven’t yet updated to [Micronaut framework 4](https://micronaut.io/2023/07/14/micronaut-framework-4-0-0-released/), this is an excellent opportunity to do so!

Please feel free to [reach out to us](https://micronaut.io/support/) if you need any assistance.
