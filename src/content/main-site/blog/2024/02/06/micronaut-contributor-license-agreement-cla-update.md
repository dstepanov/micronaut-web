Micronaut framework is so fortunate to have an engaging community with many supportive contributors. [Micronaut Core](https://github.com/micronaut-projects/micronaut-core) alone has close to 400 contributors. As a contributor to the Framework, the signature of a Contributor License Agreement is required.

With the recent partnership between [Micronaut Foundation with Unity Foundation](https://micronaut.io/2023/03/17/announcing-partnership-with-unity/), we are updating our Contributor License Agreement. Please see the [changes to the CLA in this pull request comparison](https://github.com/micronaut-projects/micronaut-cla/pull/1/files).

Please note, future contributions will require signing the updated CLA. We are so thankful to our wonderful community for the continued support and contributions to help the Micronaut framework grow and evolve.
