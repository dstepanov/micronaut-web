The Micronaut Foundation is excited to announce the release of Micronaut framework 4.6.2!

It contains patch releases of [Micronaut Core](https://github.com/micronaut-projects/micronaut-core/releases/tag/v4.6.5), [Micronaut Data](https://github.com/micronaut-projects/micronaut-data/releases/tag/v4.9.3), [Micronaut OpenAPI](https://github.com/micronaut-projects/micronaut-openapi/releases/tag/v6.12.3), [Micronaut SQL](https://github.com/micronaut-projects/micronaut-sql/releases/tag/v5.8.2), [Micronaut JaxRS](https://github.com/micronaut-projects/micronaut-jaxrs/releases/tag/v4.6.1), [Micronaut Cache](https://github.com/micronaut-projects/micronaut-cache/releases/tag/v5.0.1), [Micronaut Views](https://github.com/micronaut-projects/micronaut-views/releases/tag/v5.5.1), and [Micronaut Security.](https://github.com/micronaut-projects/micronaut-security/releases/tag/v4.10.1)

## NEXT STEPS

If you haven’t yet updated to [Micronaut framework 4](https://micronaut.io/2023/07/14/micronaut-framework-4-0-0-released/), this is an excellent opportunity to do so!

Please feel free to [reach out to us](https://micronaut.io/support/) if you need any assistance.
