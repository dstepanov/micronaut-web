The Micronaut Foundation is excited to announce the release of Micronaut framework 4.6.1!

It contains patch releases of [Micronaut Core](https://github.com/micronaut-projects/micronaut-core/releases/tag/v4.6.4), [Micronaut Data](https://github.com/micronaut-projects/micronaut-data/releases/tag/v4.9.2), and [Micronaut Servlet.](https://github.com/micronaut-projects/micronaut-servlet/releases/tag/v4.11.1)

## NEXT STEPS

If you haven’t yet updated to [Micronaut framework 4](https://micronaut.io/2023/07/14/micronaut-framework-4-0-0-released/), this is an excellent opportunity to do so!

Please feel free to [reach out to us](https://micronaut.io/support/) if you need any assistance.
