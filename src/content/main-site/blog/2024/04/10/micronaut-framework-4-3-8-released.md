The Micronaut Foundation is excited to announce the release of Micronaut framework 4.3.8!

It contains patch releases of [Micronaut Core](https://github.com/micronaut-projects/micronaut-core/releases/tag/v4.3.14), [Micronaut Security](https://github.com/micronaut-projects/micronaut-security/releases/tag/v4.6.10), and [Micronaut SQL](https://github.com/micronaut-projects/micronaut-sql/releases/tag/v5.5.2).

Moreover, if you use the [Micronaut Gradle Plugins](https://plugins.gradle.org/u/micronaut) update to 4.3.6.

## NEXT STEPS

If you haven’t yet updated to [Micronaut framework 4](https://micronaut.io/2023/07/14/micronaut-framework-4-0-0-released/), this is an excellent opportunity to do so!

Please feel free to [reach out to us](https://micronaut.io/support/) if you need any assistance.
