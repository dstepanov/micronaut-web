The Micronaut Foundation is excited to announce the release of Micronaut framework 4.4.1!

It contains patch releases of [Micronaut Core](https://github.com/micronaut-projects/micronaut-core/releases/tag/v4.4.6), [Micronaut Views](https://github.com/micronaut-projects/micronaut-views/releases/tag/v5.2.1), [Micronaut gRPC](https://github.com/micronaut-projects/micronaut-grpc/releases/tag/v4.4.1), [Micronaut Test Resources](https://github.com/micronaut-projects/micronaut-test-resources/releases/tag/v2.5.1), and [Micronaut Maven Plugin](https://github.com/micronaut-projects/micronaut-maven-plugin/releases/tag/v4.5.3).

## NEXT STEPS

If you haven’t yet updated to [Micronaut framework 4](https://micronaut.io/2023/07/14/micronaut-framework-4-0-0-released/), this is an excellent opportunity to do so!

Please feel free to [reach out to us](https://micronaut.io/support/) if you need any assistance.
