The Micronaut Foundation is excited to announce the release of Micronaut framework 4.7.2!

It contains patch releases of [Core](https://github.com/micronaut-projects/micronaut-core/releases/tag/v4.7.8), [GCP](https://github.com/micronaut-projects/micronaut-gcp/releases/tag/v5.8.1), [Data](https://github.com/micronaut-projects/micronaut-data/releases/tag/v4.10.5), [Test](https://github.com/micronaut-projects/micronaut-test/releases/tag/v4.6.2), and [Oracle Cloud](https://github.com/micronaut-projects/micronaut-oracle-cloud/releases/tag/v4.3.4).

## NEXT STEPS

If you haven’t yet updated to [Micronaut framework 4](https://micronaut.io/2023/07/14/micronaut-framework-4-0-0-released/), this is an excellent opportunity to do so!

Please feel free to [reach out to us](https://micronaut.io/support/) if you need any assistance.
