The Micronaut Foundation is excited to announce the release of Micronaut framework 4.7.3!

It contains patch releases of [Core](https://github.com/micronaut-projects/micronaut-core/releases/tag/v4.7.10), [Logging](https://github.com/micronaut-projects/micronaut-logging/releases/tag/v1.5.1), [Flyway](https://github.com/micronaut-projects/micronaut-flyway/releases/tag/v7.6.1), [Liquibase](https://github.com/micronaut-projects/micronaut-liquibase/releases/tag/v6.6.1), [Oracle Cloud](https://github.com/micronaut-projects/micronaut-oracle-cloud/releases/tag/v4.3.5), and [Pulsar](https://github.com/micronaut-projects/micronaut-pulsar/releases/tag/v2.5.1).

## NEXT STEPS

If you haven’t yet updated to [Micronaut framework 4](https://micronaut.io/2023/07/14/micronaut-framework-4-0-0-released/), this is an excellent opportunity to do so!

Please feel free to [reach out to us](https://micronaut.io/support/) if you need any assistance.
