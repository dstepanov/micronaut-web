A big thanks to everyone who joined us for the first 2GM Town Hall Meeting of 2021! We talked about a lot of great activities within the Groovy, Grails, and Micronaut communities. For those of you who missed the live event, the recording is embedded below.

Thanks go out to our panel of experts:

- James Kleeh, Micronaut Development Lead
- Paul King, Object Computing Groovy Practice Lead
- Puneet Behl, Grails Development Lead
- And our guest panelist, Graeme Rocher

We were particularly excited to celebrate the Grails framework’s 13th birthday and introduce the new Grails mascot!

![New Mascot: The Grails Knight](/micronaut-assets/main-site/wp-content/uploads/2021/02/Grails-Mascot-187x300.png)

The Grails mascot doesn’t have a name yet, and it’s up to our community to come up with one! [Complete this form](https://objectcomputing.typeform.com/to/B56M2u9X) to provide your best suggestion for a chance to win a one-of-a-kind Grails swag bag (and bragging rights, of course).

Watch the video below to see the whole meeting, and stay tuned for details about our next 2GM Town Hall meeting.

— Jen Wiese

2GM Product Evangelist
