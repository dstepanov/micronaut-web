**Introducing: [The Micronaut Podcast](https://micronautpodcast.com/)**, hosted by Micronaut Developer Advocate, Sergio del Amo.

Our first show, which covered Micronaut integration with GraalVM, was published on October 25th. Going forward, Sergio will be joined by Micronaut contributors, community members, and [Micronaut Foundation™](https://micronaut.io/foundation/) sponsors to explore a variety of topics.  Tune in every two weeks for news, tips, interviews, and more!

[Subscribe in your favorite podcast player](https://micronautpodcast.com) and let us know what topics you’d like us to cover! We look forward to your feedback.
