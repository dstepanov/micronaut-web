The Micronaut Foundation is excited to announce the release of Micronaut framework 4.10.14! It contains releases of [Micronaut Core](https://github.com/micronaut-projects/micronaut-core/releases/tag/v4.10.23) and [Micronaut Data](https://github.com/micronaut-projects/micronaut-data/releases/tag/v4.14.4).**We strongly recommend that users update to Micronaut 4.10.14**, as it ships with [Netty 4.2.13](https://github.com/netty/netty/releases/tag/netty-4.2.13.Final), which fixes multiple CVEs.

Please feel free to [reach out to us](https://micronaut.io/support/) if you need any assistance.
