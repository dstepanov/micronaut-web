The Micronaut Foundation is excited to announce the release of Micronaut framework 4.10.11! It contains releases of [Micronaut Core](https://github.com/micronaut-projects/micronaut-core/releases/tag/v4.10.20), [Micronaut Logging](https://github.com/micronaut-projects/micronaut-logging/releases/tag/v1.7.4), and [Micronaut OpenAPI.](https://github.com/micronaut-projects/micronaut-openapi/releases/tag/v6.20.0)

## NEXT STEPS

If you haven’t yet updated to [Micronaut framework 4](https://micronaut.io/2023/07/14/micronaut-framework-4-0-0-released/), this is an excellent opportunity to do so!

Please feel free to [reach out to us](https://micronaut.io/support/) if you need any assistance.
