The Micronaut Foundation is excited to announce the release of Micronaut framework 4.10.8!

It contains releases of [Micronaut Core](https://github.com/micronaut-projects/micronaut-core/releases/v4.10.15), [Micronaut Maven Plugin](https://github.com/micronaut-projects/micronaut-maven-plugin/releases/v4.11.6), [Micronaut SQL](https://github.com/micronaut-projects/micronaut-sql/releases/v6.3.3), [Micronaut Logging](https://github.com/micronaut-projects/micronaut-logging/releases/v1.7.3), [Micronaut Discovery Client](https://github.com/micronaut-projects/micronaut-discovery-client/releases/v4.7.2), and [Micronaut Data](https://github.com/micronaut-projects/micronaut-data/releases/v4.14.3).

## NEXT STEPS

If you haven’t yet updated to [Micronaut framework 4](https://micronaut.io/2023/07/14/micronaut-framework-4-0-0-released/), this is an excellent opportunity to do so!

Please feel free to [reach out to us](https://micronaut.io/support/) if you need any assistance.
