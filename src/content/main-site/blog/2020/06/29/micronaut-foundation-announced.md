Today, we announced the formation of the Micronaut Foundation, a not-for-profit company established to advance innovation and adoption of the Micronaut® framework, a free and open, public-use software development framework used to build modular, easily testable microservice and serverless applications.

The Micronaut Foundation oversees software roadmap and development, best practices and processes, repository control, documentation and support, and fundraising related to the open source framework.
