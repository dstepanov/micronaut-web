The Micronaut Foundation is excited to announce the release of Micronaut framework 4.7.4!

It contains patch releases of [Core](https://github.com/micronaut-projects/micronaut-core/releases/tag/v4.7.11), [Discovery Client](https://github.com/micronaut-projects/micronaut-discovery-client/releases/tag/v4.5.1), and [Serialization](https://github.com/micronaut-projects/micronaut-serialization/releases/tag/v2.12.1).

## NEXT STEPS

If you haven’t yet updated to [Micronaut framework 4](https://micronaut.io/2023/07/14/micronaut-framework-4-0-0-released/), this is an excellent opportunity to do so!

Please feel free to [reach out to us](https://micronaut.io/support/) if you need any assistance.
