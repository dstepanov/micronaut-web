The Micronaut Foundation is excited to announce the release of Micronaut framework 4.9.2!

It contains releases of [Micronaut Core](https://github.com/micronaut-projects/micronaut-core/releases/tag/v4.9.9), [Micronaut Security](https://github.com/micronaut-projects/micronaut-security/releases/tag/v4.13.1), [Micronaut Serialization](https://github.com/micronaut-projects/micronaut-serialization/releases/tag/v2.15.1), [Micronaut Data](https://github.com/micronaut-projects/micronaut-data/releases/tag/v4.13.3), [Micronaut JAX RS](https://github.com/micronaut-projects/micronaut-jaxrs/releases/tag/v4.9.2), [Micronaut Tracing](https://github.com/micronaut-projects/micronaut-tracing/releases/tag/v7.1.3), and [Micronaut Kubernetes 7.1.1](https://github.com/micronaut-projects/micronaut-kubernetes/releases/tag/v7.1.1).

## NEXT STEPS

If you haven’t yet updated to [Micronaut framework 4](https://micronaut.io/2023/07/14/micronaut-framework-4-0-0-released/), this is an excellent opportunity to do so!

Please feel free to [reach out to us](https://micronaut.io/support/) if you need any assistance.
