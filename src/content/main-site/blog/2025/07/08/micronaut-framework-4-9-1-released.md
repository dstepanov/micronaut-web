The Micronaut Foundation is excited to announce the release of Micronaut framework 4.9.1!

It contains releases of [Micronaut Core](https://github.com/micronaut-projects/micronaut-core/releases/v4.9.7), [Micronaut JaxRS](https://github.com/micronaut-projects/micronaut-jaxrs/releases/v4.9.1), [Micronaut Data](https://github.com/micronaut-projects/micronaut-data/releases/v4.13.2), [Micronaut SQL](https://github.com/micronaut-projects/micronaut-sql/releases/v6.2.1), [Micronaut AOT](https://github.com/micronaut-projects/micronaut-aot/releases/v2.8.1), and [Micronaut OpenAPI 6.17.3](https://github.com/micronaut-projects/micronaut-openapi/releases/v6.17.3).

## NEXT STEPS

If you haven’t yet updated to [Micronaut framework 4](https://micronaut.io/2023/07/14/micronaut-framework-4-0-0-released/), this is an excellent opportunity to do so!

Please feel free to [reach out to us](https://micronaut.io/support/) if you need any assistance.
