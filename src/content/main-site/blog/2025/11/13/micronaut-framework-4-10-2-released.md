The Micronaut Foundation is excited to announce the release of Micronaut framework 4.10.2!

It contains releases of [Micronaut MCP](https://github.com/micronaut-projects/micronaut-mcp/releases/tag/v0.0.17), [Micronaut Data](https://github.com/micronaut-projects/micronaut-data/releases/tag/v4.14.1), [Micronaut Maven Plugin](https://github.com/micronaut-projects/micronaut-maven-plugin/releases/tag/v4.11.2), [Micronaut Serialization](https://github.com/micronaut-projects/micronaut-serialization/releases/tag/v2.16.2), and [Micronaut Flyway](https://github.com/micronaut-projects/micronaut-flyway/releases/tag/v7.9.2)

## NEXT STEPS

If you haven’t yet updated to [Micronaut framework 4](https://micronaut.io/2023/07/14/micronaut-framework-4-0-0-released/), this is an excellent opportunity to do so!

Please feel free to [reach out to us](https://micronaut.io/support/) if you need any assistance.
