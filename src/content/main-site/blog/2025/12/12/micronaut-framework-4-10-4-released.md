The Micronaut Foundation is excited to announce the release of Micronaut framework 4.10.4!

It contains releases of [Micronaut Core](https://github.com/micronaut-projects/micronaut-core/releases/tag/v4.10.10), [Micronaut Maven Plugin](https://github.com/micronaut-projects/micronaut-maven-plugin/releases/tag/v4.11.4), [Micronaut MCP](https://github.com/micronaut-projects/micronaut-mcp/releases/tag/v0.0.18), and [Micronaut Micrometer](https://github.com/micronaut-projects/micronaut-micrometer/releases/tag/v5.13.2)

## NEXT STEPS

If you haven’t yet updated to [Micronaut framework 4](https://micronaut.io/2023/07/14/micronaut-framework-4-0-0-released/), this is an excellent opportunity to do so!

Please feel free to [reach out to us](https://micronaut.io/support/) if you need any assistance.
