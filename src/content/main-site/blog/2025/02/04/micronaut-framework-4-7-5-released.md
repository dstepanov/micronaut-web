The Micronaut Foundation is excited to announce the release of Micronaut framework 4.7.5!

It contains patch releases of [Core](https://github.com/micronaut-projects/micronaut-core/releases/tag/v4.7.13), [Security](https://github.com/micronaut-projects/micronaut-security/releases/tag/v4.11.3), [Validation](https://github.com/micronaut-projects/micronaut-validation/releases/tag/v4.8.1), [Maven Plugin](https://github.com/micronaut-projects/micronaut-maven-plugin/releases/tag/v4.7.2), [Kafka](https://github.com/micronaut-projects/micronaut-kafka/releases/tag/v5.7.1), [Test Resources](https://github.com/micronaut-projects/micronaut-test-resources/releases/tag/v2.7.3), and [Discovery Client](https://github.com/micronaut-projects/micronaut-discovery-client/releases/tag/v4.5.2).

## NEXT STEPS

If you haven’t yet updated to [Micronaut framework 4](https://micronaut.io/2023/07/14/micronaut-framework-4-0-0-released/), this is an excellent opportunity to do so!

Please feel free to [reach out to us](https://micronaut.io/support/) if you need any assistance.
