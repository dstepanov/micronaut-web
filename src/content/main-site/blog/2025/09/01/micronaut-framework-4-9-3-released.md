The Micronaut Foundation is excited to announce the release of Micronaut framework 4.9.3!

It contains releases of [Micronaut Core](https://github.com/micronaut-projects/micronaut-core/releases/tag/v4.9.10), [Micronaut Data](https://github.com/micronaut-projects/micronaut-data/releases/tag/v4.13.4),, and [Micronaut Spring 5.11.1](https://github.com/micronaut-projects/micronaut-spring/releases/tag/v5.11.1).

## NEXT STEPS

If you haven’t yet updated to [Micronaut framework 4](https://micronaut.io/2023/07/14/micronaut-framework-4-0-0-released/), this is an excellent opportunity to do so!

Please feel free to [reach out to us](https://micronaut.io/support/) if you need any assistance.
