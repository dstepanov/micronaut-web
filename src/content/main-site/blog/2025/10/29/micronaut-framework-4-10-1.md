The Micronaut Foundation is excited to announce the release of Micronaut framework 4.10.1!

It contains releases of [Micronaut Core 4.10.8](https://github.com/micronaut-projects/micronaut-core/releases/tag/v4.10.8), [Micronaut MCP 0.0.14](https://github.com/micronaut-projects/micronaut-mcp/releases/tag/v0.0.14), [Micronaut OpenAPI 6.19.2](https://github.com/micronaut-projects/micronaut-openapi/releases/tag/v6.19.2), [Micronaut Flyway 7.9.1](https://github.com/micronaut-projects/micronaut-flyway/releases/tag/v7.9.1), [Micronaut Test 4.10.2](https://github.com/micronaut-projects/micronaut-test/releases/tag/v4.10.2) and [Micronaut GCP 5.13.1](https://github.com/micronaut-projects/micronaut-gcp/releases/tag/v5.13.1)

## NEXT STEPS

If you haven’t yet updated to [Micronaut framework 4](https://micronaut.io/2023/07/14/micronaut-framework-4-0-0-released/), this is an excellent opportunity to do so!

Please feel free to [reach out to us](https://micronaut.io/support/) if you need any assistance.
