# Technology Advisory Board Meeting Minutes - March 12, 2021

### Board Members In Attendance:

- **Jeff Scott Brown** – Micronaut Foundation, co-founder and Director, Micronaut Practice Lead at Object Computing
- **Graeme Rocher** – Micronaut Foundation, co-founder and Director; Oracle, Architect
- **James Kleeh** – Object Computing, Micronaut Development Lead
- **Neal Ford** – ThoughtWorks, Director, Cloud Architect
- **Ken Sipe** – Mesosphere, Cloud Solutions Architect
- **Yuriy Artamonov** – JetBrains, Microservices Fellow

### Board Members Not In Attendance:

- **Guillaume LaForge** – Google, Developer Advocate for Google Cloud Platform
- **Venkat Subramaniam** – Agile Developer Inc., Founder
- **Zhamak Dehghani** – ThoughtWorks, Principal Consultant

### Others In Attendance:

- **Jen Wiese** – Object Computing, OSS Community Engagement Manager

### Meeting led by:

- Jeff Scott Brown

### Agenda:

- Welcome and Agenda
- New TAB Members
- Foundation Community Updates
- Technical Discussion
- Open Discussion

### Welcome (JSB)

- Feel free to send in topics to discuss prior to meeting at [techadvisory@micronaut.io](mailto:techadvisory@micronaut.io)
- All input and ideas are welcome
- We want to capitalize on the group of folks we have here, so please reach out

### New TAB Members (JSB)

- We are in discussions with a potential new member to add to the technology advisory board; will update the group once we know more
- If anyone has suggestions for nominations to the TAB, please send those in

### Foundation Community Updates (JW)

- As a not-for-profit organization, the Micronaut Foundation relies on the financial support from our amazing community of generous sponsors
- Corporate and Community sponsorship opportunities currently available
- We have a handful of Community All Star Sponsors
- Currently OCI is the only Corporate Sponsor
- The Foundation will be delivering events moving forward: training workshops, webinars, publications, and other online community events

### Technical Discussion (JK, All)

- Roadmap Review

  - Planning on Micronaut 3.x summer of 2021
  - Planning on Micronaut 4.x summer of 2022
- [Top Level Roadmap](https://github.com/micronaut-projects/micronaut-core/wiki/Top-Level-Roadmap)
- Default Reactive Library; Moving from RxJava2 to Project Reactor

  - Options discussed included RxJava3, Project Reactor, and Flow
- JDK Support Planning

  - Will continue to support JDK8 until Micronaut 4.x
- Migrating from Javax Injection Annotations to Jakarta Annotations

  - We will be transitioning to Jakarta Annotations in Micronaut 3.x
- Planning for a Micronaut Data repo for MongoDB

### Open Discussion (All)

- Meeting notes will be published
- Thank you to everyone who attended. Your participation and time is very much appreciated.
